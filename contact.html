Frequently Asked Questions

How can I place a order on MYSHOP website
You can click on any product which you want. It will redirect it to product detail page. There you will see a button named "Add to Cart". You can click the same and then go to the cart by clicking on cart icon available in header. In cart, you will see all your added items. You can click on Place Order button to place the order.
How can I search the specific item?
You can go to the Search tab given in header. There, in the input you can add the keyword you want to search and then either click on search icon or press the enter. You will get all the results corresponding to your keyword.
